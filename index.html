Yiling Lin

I am a computational social scientist who is interested in answering the question: where do new ideas come from? Beginning from the fall of 2021, I am a PhD student of information science at the University of Pittsburgh.

CV

Research

My research interest lies in the cultural and societal aspects of knowledge production: how does the timing for new ideas evolve with the landscape of knowing? And how are the texture of new ideas: novelty, compatibility, and substituability, etc. associated with the creativity and power of human and machine in the making of knowledge?
