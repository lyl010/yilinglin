Yiling Lin

I am a computational social scientist who is interested in understanding and measuring the innovation in knowledge. At present, I am interested in answering the question: where do new ideas come from? Beginning from the fall of 2021, I am a PhD student of information science at the University of Pittsburgh.

CV

Research

My research interest lies in the social and natural aspects of knowledge production: how does the timing for new ideas evolve with the landscape? And the texture of new ideas: how are its novelty associated with the human and automation in the making of knowledge?
